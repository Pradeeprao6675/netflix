Raath International School (RIS) has been founded in 2004 under the aegis of Raath Vidypeeth by a versatile,visionary, social activist, and industrialist Shri Balwan Singh Yadav in the vicinity of lush green Aravali hills atDughera, Behror (Alwar), later on at Alwar City, Rajasthan and after that at Kund-Rewari, Haryana (India).

Schools are the CBSE Affiliated, English Medium, 10+2, Co-educational, Day cum Residential, with Science,Commerce and Humanities stream.
